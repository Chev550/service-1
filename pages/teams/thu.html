House Description

English | 中文

WHAO House is a sustainable retire house located in northern China, targeting at a typical Chinese family of one senior couple with pre-school grandchildren, and an adult couple who visit occasionally. We propose WHAO House, an ideal living model, to meet the challenge of exceeded urbanization and overwhelming environmental problems.

The basic strategy of WHAO House is “module & cover”.

Modules are prefabricated boxes of 3m*6m and 3m*4.5m. They are functionally specialized, each covering one or several functions including bedroom, lounge, kitchen, bathroom and equipment. Modules then gather into groups to provide adequate functions for living, and a combination of three groups with eight modules is proposed in WHAO House. As the family structure changes, current layout can be developed through transformation of exterior enclosure, interior partition and furniture. Each group of modules covers a whole set of functions, which makes it possible to be sold or rent independently to individuals or families.

The cover, the outer layer of WHAO House, marks the boundary and provides a transition between privacy and publicity. Together with groups of modules, they form inner courtyards where family activities and public communication take place. The cover also contributes to a comfortable micro-climate. During summer, it is open to allow natural ventilation. Spraying device on the cover helps bring off the heat through evaporation. PV panels on the top and greening on the west façade shade the house. During winter, the cover is fully closed on the north façade to keep heat inside and prevent cold wind.

We maximize the collection of energy and resources through the use of efficient solar thermal collectors and PV panels with solar-tracking devices. Also, rainwater and grey water is gathered and recycled. On the other hand, we try to reduce consumption by using innovative energy storage material and selecting HVAC system and other appliances of high efficiency. The layout of separate zones allows shutting down air conditioning when the space is not used, and a smart energy management system is applied for more accurate and customized energy control. Activities at home are integrated by an application: with AR interface, the house becomes intelligent and responds to users’ need in lighting, safety, air conditioning, etc.

Despite self-sufficient, WHAO House is not isolated, but a component of a dynamic community and a part of a larger energy, resource and information exchanging network. It is our deliberate answer toward sustainability, living and future.

WHAO House面向老年群体而设计，是一幢位于中国北方的可持续退休住宅。

中国快速城市化进程中，传统的“家”受到挑战。为解决这一困境，WHAO House为中国典型三代家庭构想了一种居住模式：祖孙两代生活在城郊社区中的退休住宅，忙碌的中年人则在周末来此度假——这种模式是我们对于“理想住宅”的一次探索。

WHAO House的基本设计策略是“Module & Cover”。

WHAO House 包含两种模数的预制箱体模块：Module，3mx4.5m或3mx6m。模块的功能相互分立，每个模块包含卧室、起居、厨房、卫浴和设备中的一个或多个。若干箱体模块成组构成居住空间——WHAO House正是以8个箱体为居住者提供了3组居室。随着时间变化，现有的平面形式可以通过改变室外围护、室内隔断、家具布置发生改变，以应对家庭成员的结构和年龄变化。每组箱体模块都覆盖了全套生活设施，为单独出售、租赁给个人或家庭提供了可能。

WHAO House由Cover限定出家庭领域，并在公共与私密之间形成了半透明的边界。它与3组室内空间共同营造了半室外的庭院，容纳家庭活动或公共交流。Cover同时塑造了局部微气候：夏季可完全打开，自然通风和雾化降温带走庭院的多余热量，顶部光伏板和西侧绿化达到遮阳效果；冬季北侧封闭，将寒冷空气阻挡在外，也将温暖空间保持在庭院中。

WHAO House努力达到能源、资源收集的最大化：应用追光光伏系统和光热系统，同时回收利用雨水、灰水。另一方面，我们也追求更少的能源消耗：使用革新的蓄能材料，选择高效、适宜的空调和设备。此外，独立的居室空间在不使用时可完全停止供热/冷，而智能的能源管理系统也为家庭进行精确、定制化的能源管理。WHAO App应用AR技术使建筑智能地响应用户需求：在灯光、安防、空调等等方面，建筑都能与使用者发生互动。然而，尽管WHAO House能够自给自足，它并不是一幢孤岛式住宅——相反，它是活跃社区的一员，同时也是能源、资源、信息交互网络的有机组成。

面向可持续，人居，以及未来，WHAO House正是我们深思熟虑后给出的答案。
