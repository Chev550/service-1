House Description

English | 中文

Team Istanbul is working on an algae powered eco-house named Algaetech House. By using domestic waste water, this flexible house cultivates yellow and grey water for algal biomass production and wastewater treatment. Microalgeas will be produced in the “algae pool” which is placed on the roof of the house. After the desired amount of microalgae is produced, this biomass will be mixed with solid kitchen waste and brown water for methane production. Methane will be used in small scale cogeneration system and the produced energy will be supportive to meet the energy demand of the house. The main electricity energy demand of the house will be met by PV Panels. While O² production, which is 5 times more than conventional plants and trees, helps to clean the urban atmosphere, CO² will be used to feed microalgae, as an unwanted waste product of the burning.  Recycled waste water from the house is to be used in reservoirs and in the garden. Meanwhile, the resulting wastes of these wastes will be used as fertilizer for the garden. In addition, for HVAC systems there will be used different types of energy efficient strategies like Concentrated Solar Power (CSP), Evaporative Cooling, Wind Catcher with Solar Chimney and Phase Change Material. Heating-Cooling Energy demand of the building will be met with CSP and Heat Pump. Evaporative Cooling will be used to increase the efficiency of Heat Pump. Also evaporative cooling will be applied to return air in summer time. Heat recovery unit will be used to get benefit the energy of evaporatively cooled return air.

Team Istanbul focuses on a flexible architectural plan that can be designed in different modular combinations to create market alternatives. The plan can be an open plan but can also be divided per the cultural differences. Smaller and bigger square meters with additional rooms can easily be maintained within the schema. In this context, flexibility means designing a house that can be used for different climates with minor changes. For the competition, the small version of the plan for a two-person house will be produced.

伊斯坦布尔团队一直致力于设计一个名为Algaetech House的藻类动力生态屋，通过利用家庭废水，这种柔性房屋产生的黄水和灰水用于藻类的生物质生产和废水处理。微藻类将会在放置于屋顶的“藻池”中生产，当产生的微藻量达到所需量之后，该生物质与固体厨余垃圾和棕色水混合用以生产甲烷。甲烷用于小规模的热电联产系统，产生的能量将会足以满足房屋的能耗需求。房屋主要的电能需求将由光伏板提供。氧气的产生量是常规树木和植物的氧气产生量的5倍多，有助于清洁城市大气环境。二氧化碳（ CO²）作为一种燃烧时的无用产物，用于饲养微藻。可循环废水将用于水库和花园。同时，这些废物产生的废弃物会被用作花园的肥料。此外，对于HVAC（供热通风与空气调节系统），会采用不同种类的高效节能措施，比如聚光太阳能发电（CSP）、蒸发冷却、带有太阳能烟囱和相变材料的捕风装置。建筑的加热-冷却所需能量由聚光太阳能发电和热泵提供，蒸发冷却机制用于提升热泵的效率。除此之外，在夏季，蒸发冷却机制会用于回风。热回收装置将用于获取蒸发冷却回流空气产生的能量。

伊斯坦布尔团队致力于一个具有灵活性的建筑计划，它可以被设计成不同的模块化组合来创造市场替代品。它可以是一个开放性的计划也可以根据文化差异进行划分。小一些的面积和有额外的房间的大一些的面积都可以很容易的保持在这个架构之内。在这种情况下，灵活性意味着需要设计一个可以适用于有着微小变化的不同的气候。为参加本次比赛，我们将建造一个计划的缩影，一个可用于两人居住的房子。
